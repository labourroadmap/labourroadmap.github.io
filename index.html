Labour Roadmap

The Labour Roadmap project aims to support a narrative to return the Labour Party to power.

public Mission Statement

Our mission is to understand Britain today and research, derive and disseminate a pragmatic Labour vision that widely resonates and persuades the public.

The project is run by Labour members for Labour members. Our aim to is aggregate expertise in the membership to build a much needed intellectual foundation to prepare Labour's return to government.

question_answer F.A.Q.

What is your vision for Labour?
The purpose of this project is to build a considered and evolving vision for Labour with the only precondition that it must be a relevant, pragmatic and can win.
What makes you different from other Labour groups?
Labour Roadmap is a project not a group. We have no membership, we have no narrow collective ideological commitment and we do not organise within the party.
Who are you?
We are active individual Labour party members who care about putting into power a just Labour government.
What is your view on the current plight of the Labour party?
We have no view beyond that we believe the party's results are not good enough. We are focused on helping prepare Labour for government and building a positive relationship with the British people.
How can I get involved?
Best way is to contact us by email. At time to time we may release a request for specific expertise so please regularly check the website and follow us on social media.
